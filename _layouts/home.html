Introducing: Scheiner Sports Solutions

Scheiner Sports Solution is my newest creation, a site dedicated to providing valuable sports insights and analytics for sports bettors and fans. It features various betting guides such as daily pitcher props and updates such as the current status of the Tom Tango CY Young award standings. Upcoming features include an interactive prop laddering tool, current and predictive monthly wins in the MLB, and a daily "no runs first inning" (NRFI) model.
